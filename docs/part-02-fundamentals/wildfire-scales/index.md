# Wildfire Scales and Applications

## Scale Classifications

### Landscape Scale (1-100 km)
- Wildland fire spread
- Large fire management
- Fuel treatment planning

### WUI Scale (100m - 1km)  
- Structure protection
- Evacuation planning
- Defensible space

### Regional Scale (100+ km)
- Smoke transport
- Air quality impacts
- Resource allocation

## Applications by Scale

Different models and approaches are appropriate for different spatial and temporal scales.
