# Glossary

## A

**Active Crown Fire**: A fire that advances from top to top of trees or shrubs.

## B

**Burn Probability**: The likelihood that a pixel will burn given a random ignition.

## F

**FARSITE**: Fire Area Simulator - a fire behavior and growth simulation model.

**Fire Behavior Triangle**: The three factors that control fire behavior: fuels, weather, and topography.

## R

**Rate of Spread (ROS)**: The speed at which a fire moves across a landscape.

## W

**WUI**: Wildland-Urban Interface - the zone where structures and wildland fuels meet.
